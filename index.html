About Me

I do not like to write about myself, which is kind of difficult when people need to know who you are. I am a public health professional with 10 years experience working with data. I am experienced in quantiative and qualitative analysis and survey design and implemetation. In addition to research, I am also a tutor working with middle and high school students in math, science, and standardized exams. When I am not working, I can be found at home reading, completing logic puzzles, knitting, and crocheting. I am also know to be the taxicab for my family, taking them across the Northeast because, apparently it is cheaper than flying. I listen to all types of music, and even watch all genres of movies and television. I do not have favorites because it waxes and wanes like my mood.
